About Cats

Cats are fascinating creatures known for their independence, agility, and playful behavior. They have been companions to humans for thousands of years, providing love and joy in countless households. From their soft purrs to their curious antics, cats bring a unique charm to our lives.

Here are some interesting facts about cats:

  • Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  • They sleep for about 12-16 hours a day.
  • Cats have five toes on their front paws, but only four on their back paws.
  • They can jump up to six times their length!
